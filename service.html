Our Comprehensive Services

Famous Technology & Industrial Services Limited offers a full range of industrial inspection, testing, and quality assurance services to ensure safety, compliance, and operational excellence.

Lifting Equipment Inspection & Certification

Inspection and certification of cranes, forklifts, telehandlers, chain blocks, lever hoists, slings, shackles, cargo baskets, transit containers, and other lifting appliances.

Load Testing Services

Proof load testing of lifting equipment using calibrated load test equipment (up to 120 tons capacity) to ensure safe working loads and compliance.

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Comprehensive NDT services including Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI), Liquid Penetrant Inspection (LPI), Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Black Light Inspection, and Thickness Gauge Testing.

Weld Inspection Services

Professional inspection of welded joints to ensure structural integrity and compliance with industry standards including AWS, ASME, and API codes.

Tubular Goods Inspection

Thorough inspection of casing, drill pipes, bottom-hole assembly, and other tubular components for oil and gas operations.

QA/QC Services

Provision of Quality Assurance and Quality Control services for industrial and oilfield projects ensuring compliance with international standards.

Site Lifting Specialist (SLS) Services

Professional lifting supervision and compliance monitoring to ensure safe lifting operations on industrial sites.

Manpower Supply Services

Supply of certified inspection personnel and technical manpower for industrial operations with unwavering commitment to safety and professionalism.

Lifting Gear Examination & Certification

Detailed inspection and certification of slings, shackles, hooks, chains, and lifting accessories to ensure safe working conditions.

Equipment Calibration Services

Calibration of inspection and testing equipment to maintain accuracy and compliance with industry standards and regulations.

Inspection Database & Asset Tracking

Proper documentation, certification tracking, and database management for inspected equipment ensuring complete traceability.

Safety Compliance Inspection

Ensuring lifting operations and equipment comply with regulatory and safety standards including OSHA, LOLER, and local regulations.

Training Services

Comprehensive training programs including Safe Use of Lifting Equipment, Lifting Equipment Inspector Training, and Industrial Safety Training.
