About Famous Technology & Industrial Services Limited

Famous Technology & Industrial Services Limited is an independent NDT and Lifting Equipment Inspection company with our head office in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Registered on 22nd September 2008 (RC Number: 774808), we were founded by certified inspectors committed to delivering exceptional service quality and integrity.

We are flexible in our approach while always maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance. Our staff are trained to both local and international standards, ensuring we meet or exceed customer expectations in every project.

Our Mission

To be the best and most reputable oil service company, providing value-for-money quality services in NDT, Lifting Equipment Inspection, QA/QC, Tubular Goods Inspection, Rope Access Services, and Manpower Supply.

Our Vision

To become the premier lifting equipment and NDT inspection services company in the African oil and gas industry, renowned for exceptional service quality and integrity.

Our Values

Professionalism, Integrity, High Ethical Standards, and Customer Satisfaction guide everything we do.
