Who We Are

About Famous Technology & Industrial Services Limited

Famous Technology & Industrial Services Limited is an independent NDT and Lifting Equipment Inspection company with our head office in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Registered on 22nd September 2008 (RC Number: 774808), we were founded by certified inspectors committed to delivering exceptional service quality and integrity.

We are flexible in our approach while always maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance. Our staff are trained to both local and international standards, ensuring we meet or exceed customer expectations in every project.

Our Mission

To be the best and most reputable oil service company, providing value-for-money quality services in NDT, Lifting Equipment Inspection, QA/QC, Tubular Goods Inspection, Rope Access Services, and Manpower Supply.

Our Vision

To become the premier lifting equipment and NDT inspection services company in the African oil and gas industry, renowned for exceptional service quality and integrity.

Our Values

Professionalism, Integrity, High Ethical Standards, and Customer Satisfaction guide everything we do.

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our inspection services and processes.

We inspect a comprehensive range of lifting equipment including cranes, hoists, forklifts, telehandlers, chain blocks, lever hoists, slings, shackles, hooks, cargo baskets, and transit containers. Our certified inspectors ensure all equipment meets safety standards and regulatory requirements.

Our load testing services can handle equipment with capacities up to 120 tons. We use calibrated load test equipment to perform proof load testing, ensuring your lifting equipment operates safely within its rated capacity.

We provide comprehensive Non-Destructive Testing services including Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI), Liquid Penetrant Inspection (LPI), Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Black Light Inspection, and Thickness Gauge Testing. All our NDT technicians are ASNT Level II certified.

Yes, all our inspectors hold internationally recognized certifications including LEEA (Lifting Equipment Engineers Association) certification, ASNT Level II for NDT personnel, and Federal Ministry of Labour certification. We maintain ISO 9001 compliance across all operations.

Inspection duration varies depending on the type and quantity of equipment. A typical lifting equipment inspection takes 1-3 hours, while comprehensive load testing may require 4-8 hours. We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process.

Yes, we provide comprehensive inspection reports and certificates for all inspected equipment. Our reports include detailed findings, photographs, recommendations, and compliance status. We also maintain a digital database for easy tracking and retrieval of inspection records.
